Our Services

General Checkup

A general checkup is a routine health assessment to monitor overall well-being and detect potential health issues early. It includes measuring blood pressure, heart rate, BMI, and conducting basic lab tests like cholesterol and diabetes screening. Regular checkups help maintain a healthy lifestyle and prevent future complications.

Emergency Care

Emergency care provides immediate medical attention for critical conditions like heart attacks, injuries, high fever, breathing difficulties, and sudden illnesses. It ensures rapid diagnosis, first aid, and urgent treatment to stabilize patients before further medical intervention.

Blood Tests & Diagnostics

Blood tests and diagnostics help identify underlying health conditions, infections, and deficiencies. Common tests include complete blood count (CBC), liver function, kidney function, and thyroid tests. Early detection through diagnostics allows timely treatment and better health management.

Vaccinations

Vaccinations protect against infectious diseases like flu, hepatitis, COVID-19, and more. Immunization strengthens the body’s defense system, reducing the risk of severe illness. It is essential for both children and adults to stay updated on their vaccine schedules.

Telemedicine Consultation

Telemedicine allows patients to consult doctors remotely via video calls or chat, ensuring easy access to healthcare from home. It is ideal for follow-up consultations, minor illnesses, and expert medical advice without visiting a clinic physically.

Health & Wellness Programs

Health & Wellness Programs focus on promoting a healthy lifestyle through preventive care, fitness guidance, and mental well-being support. These programs include diet plans, exercise routines, stress management techniques, and chronic disease prevention

Dental Care

Dental care involves regular checkups, cleanings, and treatments to maintain oral health. It includes cavity detection, gum disease prevention, and professional cleaning to ensure strong and healthy teeth. Proper dental care prevents future oral complications and promotes overall well-being.

Eye Examination

An eye examination assesses vision health and detects issues like nearsightedness, farsightedness, and eye diseases. It includes vision tests, eye pressure measurement, and retina checks. Regular eye exams help maintain clear vision and prevent serious eye conditions.

Health Tips

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and proteins in your meals. Avoid processed foods and excess sugar to maintain a healthy body and prevent diseases.

Get Enough Sleep

Adults need 7–9 hours of sleep per night. Proper sleep improves memory, mood, and immune function. Avoid screens before bedtime for better rest.

Manage Stress

Stress can harm your health. Practice relaxation techniques like deep breathing, meditation, or spending time with loved ones to reduce stress.

Exercise Regularly

Engage in at least 30 minutes of exercise daily (walking, running, or gym workouts). Regular physical activity keeps your heart healthy.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated. Proper hydration is important for maintaining energy and good health.

Socialize and Connect

Stay connected with friends and family. Social interactions are important for mental health and emotional support.

Maintain a Healthy Weight

Strive for a healthy weight by balancing calorie intake with physical activity. Maintaining a healthy weight reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
